About abstract

One page abstract on the topic to be presented in the second part of your talk.

All abstracts will be peer-reviewed.

Important dates

  • Abstract submission deadline: 15.09.24
  • Acceptance notification: 20.10.24
  • Registration deadline: 03.11.24
  • Conference: 19.11.24 to 21.11.24

Presentation Format

Each talk shall be structured in two parts:

  1. 5 minutes to introduce yourself, your strengths and interests;
  2. 10 to 15 minutes to present the topic covered in the abstract.

Each session will consist of 4 to 5 talks on different topics, followed by 15-minute parallel discussions in break-out rooms, allowing for the delegates to choose which discussion to attend.
